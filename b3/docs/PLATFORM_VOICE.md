# Platform voice — Building Culture

Use this when writing UI, Elias replies, and marketing.

## Prefer

| Concept | Words |
|---------|--------|
| Sign in | Create your pass, Sign in |
| Rewards | Culture Points, forest credits |
| Token | Culture Dollar (BCD), community credits |
| NFT / mint | Unlock, pass, recorded |
| Platform | The forest, our community |
| Growth | Seedling → sapling → tree → grove |

## Avoid in user-facing copy

- Web3, DeFi, on-chain (use “recorded” or omit)
- Wallet connect (use “sign in”)
- Airdrop, moon, alpha
- Investment yield promises on welcome surfaces

## Themes

- **Community first** — winning together beats going alone.
- **Mother Earth** — regeneration and hubs stay visible in `/earth` and eco content.
- **Fun** — quests, streaks, and celebrations; not financial homework.
