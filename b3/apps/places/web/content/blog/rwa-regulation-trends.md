---
title: RWA regulation — patterns, not predictions
date: 2026-04-08
summary: Markets are converging on disclosure, investor protection, and segregation of duties — even when the rails are new.
---

Regulators worldwide are asking consistent questions about **tokenized securities** and **real-world assets (RWA)**: Who is the issuer? What rights does the token represent? How is custody handled? What happens in a failure scenario?

Teams building in public should expect **iterative guidance**, **sandbox programs**, and **case-by-case enforcement** while standards mature. The durable approach is to treat marketing copy as **substantiation work**: every claim should trace to a document, a contract behavior, or a clearly labeled illustration.

Nothing here predicts future rules for any jurisdiction.
