| Site | URL | In one sentence |

**Rule of thumb:** if you want to **play, mint, quest, or trade**, use **0x** or **app** (they point at the same experience). If you want **context and links**, start at **home** or **apex**.

## Follow along — three simple paths

### 1) “I’m new — where do I click?”

1. Open **[home.buildingculture.capital](https://home.buildingculture.capital/)** for the narrative and ecosystem map.  
2. When you’re ready to connect a wallet, open **[0x.buildingculture.capital](https://0x.buildingculture.capital/)** (or **[app.buildingculture.capital](https://app.buildingculture.capital/)**).  
3. Explore **Mission**, **drops**, **marketplace**, and **FAQ** inside the app footer.

### 2) “I care about places, hubs, and revival”

1. Visit **[eco.buildingculture.capital](https://eco.buildingculture.capital/)** for the eco storyline.  
2. Use **Marketplace ↗** from there when you want on-chain listings and tickets in the BUILDCHAIN app.

### 3) “I’m an investor or reader — not minting today”

1. **[buildingculture.capital](https://buildingculture.capital/)** — apex positioning.  
2. **[home.buildingculture.capital](https://home.buildingculture.capital/)** — broader ecosystem view.  
3. Still curious about product mechanics? Skim **[FAQ](https://0x.buildingculture.capital/faq)** on the app.
